## What LifeOS Is

LifeOS is an AI-native operating system for lifelong intellectual,
personal, and spiritual formation. It exists to take the raw material of a
life — books, articles, notes, conversations, reflections, and lived
experience — and turn it into organized knowledge, an evolving worldview
synthesis, and a practical, integrated way of living.

It is built for a single user. It is not a note-taking app, a to-do app,
or a chatbot wrapper — it is the substrate a person uses to think, learn,
and become who they are trying to become, over years, not sessions.

## What It Does

LifeOS takes in:

- Books, articles, and other reading material
- Notes, quotes, and highlights
- Conversations and dialogues (including with AI)
- Reflections, journaling, and lived experience

...and turns that input into:

- Organized, categorized, retrievable knowledge
- An evolving synthesis of the user's worldview — not just storage, but
  connection and sense-making across sources and time
- Practical formation — knowledge that changes how the user actually
  lives, not just what they know

## Core Capabilities

The following capabilities describe the intended shape of the product.
None of them are built yet — Sprint 1 is foundation only. Order here is
not a commitment to build order; see `PROJECT_MEMORY.md` §5 (Feature
Roadmap) for sequencing.

- **Scanning & capture** — getting material (text, quotes, ideas) into the
  system with minimal friction
- **Compiling & categorizing** — organizing raw captures into structured,
  navigable knowledge
- **Organizing & note-taking** — a durable home for notes that connects to
  everything else, not an isolated app
- **Quote saving** — preserving exact language from sources, attributable
  and searchable
- **Megathreads** — long-running, evolving threads of thought on a topic
  that accumulate over time rather than resetting each session
- **Knowledge graphs** — explicit, navigable connections between ideas,
  sources, and reflections, not just a flat list of notes
- **Constitution Engine** — the synthesis layer that turns accumulated
  knowledge and reflection into an articulated, living set of principles
  and practices the user actually operates by — the mechanism by which
  "knowledge" becomes "a way of life"

## Who It's For

A single user, using LifeOS as a long-term personal instrument. Not
multi-tenant, not a team tool, not a public product surface (today).

## Explicit Non-Goals (for now)

- Not a general-purpose PKM tool for arbitrary users
- Not a social or collaborative product
- Not trying to replace reading/thinking with AI summarization — the AI's
  role is to help organize, connect, and surface, not to substitute
  judgment
